Six ways to
own your name.

Jackson, none of this is a pitch, and nobody is trying to sign you or take a piece of what you built.

I made these six sites for fun, using only your real work: your covers, your moves, your mic, your wall, your story. Under each one I wrote down the actual thinking, the colors, the layout, the assets, and the one bold move, so you can see how it is done and go build it yourself.

You invented the move. You made the sound. You can own the whole thing. No label, no middle-man, no one taking a cut of a thing you made with your own body. That is the point. Keep making your art. Just keep it yours.

Shane

The real offer

And I mean this: if you want, I will sit down with you and show you exactly how to do every single thing I just did here, start to finish. Not do it for you, teach you to do it yourself, so your creative is fully in your own hands. No catch, no cut.

I already started it for you. Built you an academy: how to run your own sites with Claude Code, the money, the systems, the whole thing, in plain words, in your corner.
